Shakshuka

This shakshuka recipe is a one-dish meal that is fast, easy, and delicious. Be sure to cook the sauce until the veggies are nice, soft, and sweet.

  • large onion
  • fresh mushrooms
  • red bell pepper
  • cumin
  • paprika
  • cayenne pepper
  • 6 large eggs
  • crumbled feta cheese
